Leadership is incidental to the movement

While the Delano Grape Strike is known for Cesar Chavez's leadership and considered a Chicano movement, Filipinos played a pivotal role.

The strike against the grape growers of Delano, California, was a multiethnic alliance that began with Larry Itliong and would prosper with the help of Philip Vera Cruz and Pete Velasco.
